# FuseIQ — Open source boundary

FuseIQ uses a **hybrid** model: we open the **edges**, we keep the **platform** proprietary.

## Private (proprietary) — hosted at [fuseiq.io](https://fuseiq.io)

The platform monorepo and production stack stay closed to protect:

- Multi-tenant billing (Paddle) and marketplace payouts
- Workspace isolation (Supabase RLS)
- Integration OAuth credentials and dispatch
- Swarm execution engine, Eval Studio, Agent Wallet
- Agency white-label and partner CRM

We are **not** competing on self-hosting the full stack. We compete on **governed hosted orchestration** — Proof Loop, HITL, and BYOK economics in one product.
